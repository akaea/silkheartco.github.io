About Us

Silk Heart Company specializes in offering a wide variety of high-quality silks hijabs to our main customers which is women. Our business was based on Selangor Darul Ehsan. The main reason why our company chose silk as the main fabric is because of its luxurious feel, comfort and timeless elegance. The other reason why silk became our main fabric because of lightweight and breathable while ensuring comfort throughout the day. By using silk, we aim to provide a high-quality hijab that not only looks beautiful but also offers superior comfort and practicality for everyday wear.

Vision

Become a leading brand in the hijab industry, known for our commitment to elegance, comfort and innovation

Mission

Provide exquisitely crafted silk hijab that empower women to express their individual while honoring their cultural heritage

Objectives

Achieve the highest levels of customer satisfaction throughout exceptional products and outstanding service and continually expanding our range to meet the evolving needs of our client
